Why TEDxStockholm?

The Swedish welfare system, a world-class educational system and a non-hierarchical working environment have enabled Sweden to be a country ranking high on innovation and Stockholm as an emerging capital of innovation. The city is blessed with a strong creative and innovative mindset -the Stockholm mindset. Today, however, Stockholm is lacking a natural open forum where people can meet and ideas worth spreading can evolve. There is also a lack of insight in the true value and potential of the Stockholm mindset. TEDxStockholm is our way of creating such a forum and spreading this insight.

Our purpose

We want to further promote the innovative and transparent nature of the Stockholm mindset to make Stockholm the capital of innovation. At the same time we want to act as a facilitator for knowledge-sharing by bringing the TED mindset to Stockholm. We believe this will be achieved by creating a physical forum, an open think tank, where curious and open-minded individuals can meet and let ideas and concepts evolve, take tangible form and come alive!

TEDx is a 21st century place for the domains of education, innovation, artistry, design, science and entrepreneurship where these concepts are allowed to continuously evolve and be re-imagined into newer and ever changing forms.
